How to Use 7-Eleven Testing Round

Quick guide to get the most out of your shopping experience

01
Register

Click on Register, fill in the form with your details and create your account. It's free and quick.

02
Sign In

Use your email and password to access your account and start shopping.

03
Browse & Search

Navigate categories or use the search bar to find products. Filter by price, discount and availability.

04
Add to Cart

Go to a product, review the details and press Add to Cart. You can adjust quantities.

05
Pay & Receive

Fill in your address, choose Cash on Delivery (COD) or Online Payment and confirm your order. Done!
